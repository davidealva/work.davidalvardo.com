Boise Art Museum Concept

This is a concept that was requested for the redesign of the Boise Art Museum website. The current website is not fully responsive on many devices and is in need of addressing some serious flaws in the user experience and overall usability. Analytics and feedback from user testing show that the most common items such as "search" as well as information regarding upcoming events are the most pressing items that need to be addressed.

The current site offers very little in the way of easily accessing the most relevent information that most users are frequently looking for. The design is also meant to refreash and give a more visually exciting and engaging homepage that one would expect from a museum. This concept highlights my approach to design from a minimalist point of view, using strong, clean elements to bring attention to the most relevent information without over cluttering the page as well as taking a responsive/mobile first approach.

  • Client: BAM Concept Prototype
  • Date: October 2017
  • Role: Prototyping, UX UI Design, Front End Development
  • Technologies: Sketch to HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Javascript, Node.js, Gulp

Porter Guitars Configurator

Porter Guitars is a Boise based manufacturer of custom guitars and pickups. I was contracted to take the role of Front End Developer on the project, building out a configurator that allows you to custom build your guitar with components of your choice.

The work started with converting a PSD mockup to HTML5/Bootstrap utilizing Vue.js to build components to create the various functions needed to build out the guitar. My role consisted of completing 80% of the front end development and handing it off to the backend developer to complete the needed connection of the app to the API and database.

  • Technologies: PSD to HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Javascript, Vue.js, jSON, Node.js, Gulp
